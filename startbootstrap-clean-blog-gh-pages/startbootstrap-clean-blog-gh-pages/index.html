Missions in Alaska

A Guide for all Incoming AAM Missionaries

What You Will Need

  • Parka
  • Hot Hands
  • Good Suitcases (You will probably fly a good amount)
  • Happy Light
  • LAYERS!!!!!
  • A Church-approved phone with a good camera (Trust me)



Where You Could Serve

  • Anchorage
  • Fairbanks
  • Juneau
  • Eagle River
  • Palmer
  • The Southeast Islands
  • Soldotna
  • Many other smaller villages



What You Could See

  • Moose
  • Glaciers
  • Eagles
  • More Moose
  • Whales (From the shore of course)
  • Many other excited wildlife and landscapes



My Promise:

If you serve with all you have, you will leave with no regrets. You will also find you have had a great time and you have made some memories you will never forget with some people who will be some of your best friends forever.
